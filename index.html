WHAT IS KICKSTAND?

Kickstand was made for Detroiters who take socializing and biking seriously, but don't take themselves too seriously. Browse through our pre-planned bike routes that will satisfy any craving for a snack or scene. Sign into our app using Facebook for special offers and discounts to enjoy during your ride.
